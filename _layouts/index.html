** Update February 2019 **

Friends of Hume Texts Online may wish to visit our larger project, English Philosophical Texts Online, the aim of which is to collect together the best digital editions of a broad canon of English philosphical works from Hobbes to Mill. These texts will all be freely available, alongside user-friendly tools for performing sophisticated searches, textual comparisons, and stylometric analyses. Head over to englishphilosophy.org for more information.
