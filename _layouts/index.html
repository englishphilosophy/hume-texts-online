** Update November 2018 **

We are delighted to announce that the text for Hume's History of England is finally available, meaning that we now have a complete set of everything Hume published. The inclusion of this work coincides (not coincidentally) with the start of a new project, English Philosophical Texts Online, the aim of which is to collect together the best digital editions of a broad canon of English philosphical works from Hobbes to Mill. These texts will be made freely available on the web, alongside user-friendly tools for performing sophisticated searches, textual comparisons, and stylometric analyses. For more information, watch this space, or email amyas.merivale@philosophy.ox.ac.uk.
