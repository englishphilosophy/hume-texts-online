Show your support

We are currently putting together an application to the Leverhulme Trust for funding to complete our larger-scale project English Philosophical Texts Online. If you appreciate these resources and would like to see them grow, please consider sending one or two sentences of support (together with your name and institutional affiliation, if any) to amyas.merivale@philosophy.ox.ac.uk. With your permission, we would like to include these responses on our sites or in our application.
