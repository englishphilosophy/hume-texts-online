Papers about David Hume

Papers by the creators of this website (Prof. Peter Millican and Dr Amyas Merivale) are collected here, and we also store a few other papers that are hard to obtain elsewhere, so as to make them easily available to Hume scholars. Please contact us if you have recommendations for other papers that could usefully be added to this collection (subject to appropriate copyright agreements).
