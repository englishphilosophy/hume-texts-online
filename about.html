--- section: about ---

Welcome to davidhume.org, a free and open access collection of almost everything written by the great Enlightenment thinker, David Hume, together with bibliographies and links to Hume scholarship on the web. The site is developed and maintained by Amyas Merivale, and the texts were prepared by Amyas Merivale and Peter Millican. Images of Hume's manuscript of the Dialogues are provided with permission from the National Library of Scotland.
